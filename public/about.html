About

WitsGo is an innovative campus navigation and transportation platform developed to address the pressing mobility challenges faced by students, faculty, and visitors at Wits Main Campus. With its sprawling and intricate layout, navigating the campus can be a frustrating experience, particularly for first-year students and newcomers. WitsGo is designed to simplify campus travel by offering comprehensive maps, real-time updates, and intelligent route optimization, ensuring users can find their way with ease and efficiency.

The app provides real-time GPS tracking of both users and transportation services, allowing for better travel planning and decision-making. WitsGo's advanced route optimization algorithms suggest the fastest routes based on current conditions, and its unified system integrates various transportation options, including buses and rentals, giving users more flexibility in how they travel around campus. By streamlining travel, WitsGo eliminates the frustration of missed rides and inefficient routes.

In addition, WitsGo is committed to creating an inclusive experience for all users, including those with disabilities. With designated accessibility features and user-friendly interface design, the app ensures that navigating the campus is both intuitive and accessible for everyone. By addressing critical issues like limited transportation options, inefficient rentals, and accessibility barriers, WitsGo revolutionizes how users interact with the campus environment.
